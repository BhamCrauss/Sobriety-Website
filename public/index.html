About This Project


This project was started in hopes of helping individuals struggling with alcoholism and addiction, as well as, sharing insights to those who love or work with them. The problem of alcoholism and addiction is nothing new, but in the midst of the opioid epidemic, we feel that there is still plenty of work that needs to be done.

The survey used to gather the data used for this project was developed to be open-minded to all types of recovery processes and individuals. The survey is especially well suited to addicts and alcoholics who are currently sober or who have been sober in the past. We encourage you to participate in the survey if you have ever had a problem with alcoholism or addiction. Please feel free to share the link to this site and the survey!
