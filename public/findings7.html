Prescription Assistance

The vast majority of our respondents did not use prescriptions to aid in the early recovery process. Out of the five prescriptions asked about in our survey, Vivitrol and Suboxone were the most used. Vivitrol is used to prevent alcohol and opioid use. Suboxone is used to prevent opioid use.

Most respondents used suboxone for less than a month. Vivtrol has a wider range of use time. Respondents reported using it for a month to over a year.

These results show that using presciption aid for the early recovery process has been used successfully in the sense that respondents were able to form a sustainable recovery process.
